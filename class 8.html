Geometry

Exterior Angle Of A Triangle

An exterior angle of a triangle is created by extending one side of the triangle. The angle between the extended side and the side next to it is called an exterior angle

Angles In Parallel Lines And Intersecting Lines

When a transversal intersects with two parallel lines eight angles are produced. The eight angles will together form four pairs of corresponding angles. Angles 1 and 5 constitutes one of the pairs.

Midpoint Of A Line Segment

In geometry, the midpoint is the middle point of a line segment. It is equidistant from both endpoints, and it is the centroid both of the segment and of the endpoints. It bisects the segment.
